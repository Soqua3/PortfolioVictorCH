Ball Game

Main tasks in this project was to gain experience with Unity 3D and create your own game with two different levels. The essence of the game is reach to finish and collect as many points as possible. first is launching menu window, where the button for starting the game, after this player start the first level. on the first level, the player will need to find a platform that opens the door and then touch the red pillar, which will take palyer to the second level. At the second level, the player will need to use a jump to reach the finish. The photo above shows the level design. I will leave a link to the source code.(Click me)
